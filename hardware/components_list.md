# Hardware Components List

## Main Components

- Arduino Uno Rev3  
- L298N Motor Driver Module  
- DC Motor GA25-370 with Encoder (12V, 130 RPM)  

---

## Power

- 12V DC Power Adapter  

---

## Wiring

- Jumper Wires (Male-to-Male / Male-to-Female)  

---

## Mechanical Components

- 3D Printed Pointer (PLA)  
- Protractor (for angle measurement)  

---

## Notes

- The encoder is integrated with the motor  
- External power supply is used to drive the motor through L298N  
- Arduino is powered separately via USB
