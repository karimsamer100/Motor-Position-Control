# 3D Printed Pointer Design

## Description
This pointer is designed to be mounted on the DC motor shaft to visually indicate the angular position.  
It is used together with a protractor for measuring the output angle of the motor.

---

## Shaft Specifications (Measured)

- Shaft Diameter: 4 mm  
- Flat Cut Length: 2.6 mm  

---

## Pointer Design Specifications

- Hole Diameter: 5 mm  
- Flat Cut Size: 2.8 mm  
- Total Pointer Length: 2.6 cm  

---

## Design Considerations

The pointer dimensions were intentionally designed slightly larger than the motor shaft dimensions to account for:

- 3D printing tolerances  
- Material shrinkage  
- Assembly ease  

This ensures that the part fits without excessive force.

---

## Mounting Method

Due to the tolerance gap between the shaft and the pointer:

- Super glue or adhesive is used to secure the pointer firmly  
- This prevents slipping during motor rotation  

---

## Printing Settings

- Material: PLA  
- Infill: 20%  
- Layer Height: 0.2 mm  
- Supports: Not required  

---

## Notes

- A slightly loose fit is preferred over a tight fit to avoid damaging the shaft  
- Proper alignment is required before fixing the pointer permanently
